<p><p>The Aircraft Fuel Containment Market is divided into two main types: flexible and rigid fuel containment systems. Flexible fuel containment involves products like fuel bladders and liners that can adapt to various aircraft shapes, offering lightweight solutions ideal for smaller aircraft. Rigid fuel containment, on the other hand, consists of fixed tanks made from durable materials, providing high structural integrity, commonly used in larger aircraft. Both types aim to enhance fuel safety and efficiency while adhering to aviation regulations.</p></p>
