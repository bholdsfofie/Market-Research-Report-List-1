<p><p>The thermal control system market encompasses three primary types of cameras based on wavelength: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave infrared cameras. Short-wave cameras capture thermal signatures in the 0.9 to 3 microns range, ideal for high-temperature applications and industrial inspections. Mid-wave cameras, operating between 3 to 5 microns, are commonly used in defense and surveillance. Long-wave cameras, ranging from 8 to 14 microns, excel in low-temperature and night vision applications, making them essential for security and building inspection.</p></p>
