<p><p>The industrial furnaces and ovens market is divided into two primary types: combustion and electric. They are typically used for high-temperature applications and offer cost-effectiveness for large-scale operations. In contrast, electric-type furnaces use electric currents to produce heat, providing precise temperature control and cleaner operations.
