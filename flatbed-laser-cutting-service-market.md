<p><ul><li>CO2 Laser Cutting</li><li>Fiber Optic Laser Cutting</li><li>Waterjet Cutting</li><li>Plasma Cutting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flatbed laser cutting services encompass various techniques used for precision material processing. CO2 laser cutting utilizes carbon dioxide gas to produce a highly focused beam, ideal for cutting non-metal materials. Fiber optic laser cutting leverages fiber lasers for efficient metal cutting with high precision. Waterjet cutting employs high-pressure water mixed with abrasives, suitable for delicate and heat-sensitive materials. Plasma cutting uses ionized gas for high-speed metal cutting, effectively handling thicker materials. Each method is tailored to specific applications and material types.</p></p>
