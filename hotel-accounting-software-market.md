<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hotel accounting software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of access from anywhere with an internet connection. This model typically involves subscription pricing and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on the hotel's own servers, providing full control over data security and operations, but usually requires a higher upfront investment and ongoing maintenance costs.</p></p>
