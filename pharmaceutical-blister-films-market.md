<p><p>The pharmaceutical blister films market encompasses various materials used for packaging solid dosage forms. PCTFE (Polychlorotrifluoroethylene) offers excellent moisture barrier properties, while PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride) ensures high barrier protection against oxygen and moisture. COC (Cyclo Olefin Copolymer) cold form foil provides robust mechanical properties and seal integrity. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is widely used for its cost-effectiveness and versatility, and PP (Polypropylene) is valued for its lightweight and heat resistance. Each material serves distinct packaging needs based on product stability and shelf life requirements.</p></p>
