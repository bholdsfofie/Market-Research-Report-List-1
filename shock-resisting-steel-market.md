<p><p>Shock-resisting steels, such as S1, S2, S5, S6, and S7, are specialized alloys designed to withstand high-impact and shock-loading conditions. S1 steel offers moderate toughness and wear resistance, suitable for general-purpose tools. S2 steel provides enhanced toughness and is often used in chisels and hammers. S5 steel excels in high-impact applications like jackhammer bits. S6 steel is ideal for pneumatic tools due to its superior shock absorption. S7 steel is the most durable, offering exceptional toughness and resistance to cracking, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications like forging dies.</p></p>
