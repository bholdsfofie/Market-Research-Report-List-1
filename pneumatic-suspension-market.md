<p><ul><li>Capsule</li><li>Membrane</li><li>Compound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic suspension market includes three primary types: capsule, membrane, and compound systems. Capsule types use rigid air-filled chambers to provide support and absorb shocks, offering a stable ride. Membrane suspensions utilize flexible membranes that inflate or deflate, allowing for adjustable stiffness and improved comfort. Compound systems combine elements of both capsule and membrane technologies, optimizing performance and adaptability. Together, these types cater to diverse applications in automotive, industrial, and recreational vehicles, enhancing ride quality and load management.</p></p>
