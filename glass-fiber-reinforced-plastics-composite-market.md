<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Plastic</li><li>Thermosetting Plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass Fiber Reinforced Plastics (GFRP) composite market is primarily divided into two types: thermoplastic and thermosetting plastics. Thermoplastic composites can be reshaped and recycled when heated, offering versatility and ease of processing. They exhibit excellent impact resistance and are often used in automotive and consumer goods. In contrast, thermosetting composites harden permanently after curing, providing superior strength and thermal resistance, making them ideal for aerospace, construction, and industrial applications. Each type serves distinct industries with unique performance requirements.</p></p>
