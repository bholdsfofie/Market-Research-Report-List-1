<p><ul><li>Single Roller Pump</li><li>Double Roller Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Heart-Lung Machines market comprises two main types: Single Roller Pumps and Double Roller Pumps. Single Roller Pumps utilize a single roller mechanism to facilitate blood flow and oxygenation during cardiac surgery, offering simplicity and reliability. Conversely, Double Roller Pumps employ two rollers, enhancing flow consistency and reducing shear stress on blood cells. Both types are critical for cardiac bypass procedures, with the choice between them often influenced by specific surgical requirements and the desired level of control over blood circulation.</p></p>
