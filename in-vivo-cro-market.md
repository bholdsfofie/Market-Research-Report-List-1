<p><p>The in vivo contract research organization (CRO) market is divided primarily into two categories: rodent-based and non-rodent-based models. Rodent-based models encompass studies conducted using mice and rats, which are widely used due to their genetic similarity to humans and established biological understanding. Both types of models play crucial roles in drug development and safety assessment.</p></p>
