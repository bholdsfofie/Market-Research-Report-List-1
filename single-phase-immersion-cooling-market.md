<p><p>The single-phase immersion cooling market can be categorized into water-based and oil-based systems. Water-based immersion cooling utilizes water due to its high thermal conductivity and availability, efficiently maintaining optimal temperatures for electronic components. Conversely, oil-based immersion cooling employs specially formulated oils that provide excellent heat transfer properties and insulation, making them suitable for high-performance computing. Both types aim to enhance cooling efficiency, extend equipment lifespan, and reduce energy costs in data centers and electronic applications.</p></p>
