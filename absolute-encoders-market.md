<p><p>The absolute encoders market is divided into two primary types: solid shaft and hollow shaft encoders. Solid shaft encoders feature a single, rigid shaft that directly transmits motion, making them ideal for applications with limited space and simpler assembly. In contrast, hollow shaft encoders have a central opening that allows them to fit over existing shafts, offering flexibility and easier integration into complex systems. Both types serve critical roles in industrial automation, robotics, and machinery positioning, catering to diverse application needs.</p></p>
