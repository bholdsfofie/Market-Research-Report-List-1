<p><ul><li>Internal Spline</li><li>External Spline</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spline bits are specialized drilling tools featuring either internal or external splines. Internal spline bits have teeth on the interior, allowing them to fit snugly onto tools or components with matching splines, ensuring efficient torque transfer. Conversely, external spline bits possess teeth on the outside, designed to engage with corresponding internal components. The market for these bits is driven by applications in industries such as oil and gas, mining, and construction, where precision and durability are critical for performance.</p></p>
