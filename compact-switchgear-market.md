<p><p>The compact switchgear market is categorized into three main types: high voltage switchgear, medium voltage switchgear, and low voltage switchgear. High voltage switchgear is used for voltages above 36 kV, suitable for large-scale power distribution in substations. Medium voltage switchgear operates within the range of 1 kV to 36 kV, commonly utilized in industrial and commercial applications for efficient power management. Low voltage switchgear, operating below 1 kV, is essential for controlling and protecting electrical circuits in residential and small commercial settings.</p></p>
