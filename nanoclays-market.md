<p><p>The nanoclays market is segmented into types like Kaolinite, Smectite, and Others. Kaolinite nanoclays are known for their layered structure and are widely used in ceramics, paper, and coatings due to their stability and low cost. Smectite nanoclays, particularly montmorillonite, are highly absorbent and used in polymer nanocomposites, drilling fluids, and environmental applications. The "Others" category includes less common nanoclays like halloysite and sepiolite, which find niche applications in drug delivery, cosmetics, and advanced materials due to their unique properties.</p></p>
