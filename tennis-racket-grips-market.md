<p><p>The tennis racket grips market is divided into two main types: overgrips and replacement grips. Tennis racket overgrips are thin layers applied over the existing grip, enhancing comfort, moisture absorption, and control without altering the racket's original grip size. In contrast, tennis racket replacement grips are thicker and serve as the primary grip, replacing the original grip entirely. Both types cater to players' preferences for feel and performance, affecting gameplay and overall racket handling.</p></p>
