<p><p>Medium Density Fibre Board (MDF) is an engineered wood product made by breaking down hardwood or softwood residuals into wood fibers, combining them with wax and resin, and forming panels under high temperature and pressure. MDF is widely used in furniture, cabinetry, flooring, and decorative applications due to its smooth surface, uniform density, and ease of machining. It is a cost-effective alternative to solid wood and plywood, offering versatility in design and finish.</p><p>The Medium Density Fibre Board Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 2.00% during the forecast period. Overall, the MDF market is poised for steady growth, supported by its widespread applications and evolving consumer preferences.</p></p>
