<p><p>Cancer testing kits encompass various types designed for early detection and monitoring of different cancers. The FIT test screens for colorectal cancer via stool samples. HPV tests detect high-risk types of the virus linked to cervical cancer. The PSA test measures prostate-specific antigen levels for prostate cancer risk. The AFP test assesses alpha-fetoprotein for liver cancer detection, while the CEA test measures carcinoembryonic antigen for colorectal and other cancers. Other tests may include tumor markers and genetic assessments for personalized screening approaches.</p></p>
