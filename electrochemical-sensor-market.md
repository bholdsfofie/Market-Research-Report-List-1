<p><p>The electrochemical sensor market encompasses three main types: potentiometric, amperometric, and conductometric sensors. Potentiometric sensors measure voltage changes to determine ion concentration, making them suitable for pH and ion-selective applications. Amperometric sensors detect current changes resulting from electrochemical reactions, commonly used in glucose and gas analysis. Conductometric sensors assess conductivity changes in solutions, suitable for monitoring salinity and contamination levels. Each type has distinct applications across industries, including environmental monitoring, healthcare, and food safety.</p></p>
