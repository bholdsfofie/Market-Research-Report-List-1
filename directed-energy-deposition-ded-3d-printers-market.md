<p><p>Directed Energy Deposition (DED) 3D printers utilize focused energy sources to melt materials and create components layer by layer. The market consists of three primary types: Laser DED employs high-energy lasers for precision and versatility, ideal for metals and alloys. Electron Beam DED uses an electron beam in a vacuum, offering superior penetration and efficiency for large parts. Plasma Arc DED utilizes plasma to melt materials, allowing for the deposition of various metals, particularly beneficial in thicker substrates.</p></p>
