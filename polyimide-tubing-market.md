<p><p>The polyimide tubing market features distinct types, primarily seamless tubing and spiral wound tubing. Seamless tubing is characterized by its continuous structure, providing superior strength, flexibility, and resistance to high temperatures, making it ideal for aerospace and electronics applications. In contrast, spiral wound tubing consists of bonded layers, offering enhanced durability and chemical resistance, making it suitable for industries requiring complex geometries and fluid transport. Both types cater to diverse applications, leveraging polyimide's inherent thermal and mechanical properties.</p></p>
