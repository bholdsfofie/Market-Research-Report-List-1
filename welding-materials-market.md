<p><p>The welding materials market comprises electrode and filler materials, fluxes and wires, and gases, each essential for different welding processes. Electrodes and fillers provide the necessary material to create strong joints, with electrodes conducting current and fillers melting to form the weld. Fluxes and wires are used in processes like MIG and TIG welding, where fluxes clean surfaces and wires act as filler material. Gases, such as argon and CO2, shield the weld from contamination, ensuring high-quality, durable welds across industries.</p></p>
