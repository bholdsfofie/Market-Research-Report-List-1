<p><p>Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy (DMD) treatments primarily include Exondys 51, Emflaza, and Translarna. Exondys 51 is designed to skip specific exons in the dystrophin gene to enhance protein production, targeting a subset of DMD patients. Emflaza, a corticosteroid, reduces inflammation and slows muscle degeneration, benefiting all DMD patients. Translarna promotes the production of dystrophin in patients with stop codon mutations, enabling continued muscle function. Each medication addresses unique genetic and physiological aspects of DMD, expanding therapeutic options for affected individuals.</p></p>
