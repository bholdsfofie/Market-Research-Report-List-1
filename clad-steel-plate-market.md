<p><strong>The Clad Steel Plate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Explosion Bonded</li><li>Roll Bonded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Clad steel plates are composite materials made by bonding dissimilar metals to achieve enhanced properties. The explosion bonded type utilizes a high-energy explosion to create a metallurgical bond, resulting in strong and durable plates suitable for extreme conditions. In contrast, the roll bonded type involves the mechanical pressing of layers at high temperatures to achieve bonding, ideal for applications needing lighter, flexible, and cost-effective materials. Both types cater to specialized industries such as oil and gas, shipbuilding, and construction.</p></p>
