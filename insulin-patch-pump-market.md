<p><p>The insulin patch pump market consists of three main types: basal insulin, bolus insulin, and basal-bolus insulin. Basal insulin provides a constant delivery of insulin throughout the day, helping maintain stable glucose levels. Bolus insulin is used for mealtime control, allowing users to manage spikes in blood sugar by delivering insulin at specific times. Basal-bolus insulin combines both approaches, offering flexibility for patients to tailor their insulin dosing according to their daily activities and dietary intake, thus optimizing diabetes management.</p></p>
