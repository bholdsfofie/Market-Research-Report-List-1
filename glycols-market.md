<p><p>The glycol market can be segmented into three main types: pharmaceutical grade, food grade, and industrial grade. Food grade glycols are safe for consumption and utilized in food processing and additives. Industrial grade glycols are used in various industrial applications, including antifreeze, coolant formulations, and manufacturing processes, and may not meet the same purity standards as the other two grades. Each type caters to distinct industry requirements.</p></p>
