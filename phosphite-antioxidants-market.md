<p><p>Phosphite antioxidants are chemical compounds widely used as stabilizers in polymers, plastics, and other materials to prevent oxidative degradation. They function by neutralizing free radicals and decomposing hydroperoxides, thereby extending the lifespan and maintaining the performance of materials exposed to heat, light, and oxygen. These antioxidants are particularly valued in industries such as packaging, automotive, and construction due to their effectiveness and cost-efficiency.</p><p>The Phosphite Antioxidants Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period.
