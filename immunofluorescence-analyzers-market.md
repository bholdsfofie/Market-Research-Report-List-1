<p><strong>The Immunofluorescence Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Immunoassay</li><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li><li>Radioimmunoassay</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Immunofluorescence analyzers utilize various assay types to detect and quantify specific proteins or antibodies in samples. Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Immunoassays (ELFIA) employ enzymes to produce a fluorescent signal, while Chemiluminescence Immunoassays (CLIA) utilize light-emitting chemical reactions. Radioimmunoassays (RIA) rely on radioactive isotopes for detection, whereas Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA) use enzyme-substrate reactions to generate measurable signals. Each method offers unique advantages in sensitivity, specificity, and application across diagnostics and research.</p></p>
