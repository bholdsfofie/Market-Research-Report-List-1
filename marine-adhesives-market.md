<p><p>The marine adhesives market features three primary types: epoxy, acrylic, and polyurethane. Epoxy adhesives offer exceptional strength and durability, making them ideal for bonding metals, wood, and composites in harsh marine environments. Acrylic adhesives provide flexibility and fast curing times, suitable for a variety of materials and resistant to UV exposure. Polyurethane adhesives are known for their versatility and excellent moisture resistance, often used in applications requiring elasticity and shock absorption. Each type serves specific needs in maritime applications.</p></p>
