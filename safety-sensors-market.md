<p><p>The safety sensors market encompasses various types, including safety light curtains, safety laser scanners, and single-beam safety sensors. Safety laser scanners utilize laser technology to create a protective field, allowing for flexible monitoring of dynamic areas. Single-beam safety sensors consist of a single beam that triggers safety measures when interrupted, offering a compact solution for safeguarding specific zones. Each type enhances workplace safety by preventing accidents.</p></p>
