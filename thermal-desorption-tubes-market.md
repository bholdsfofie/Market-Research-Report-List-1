<p><p>The thermal desorption tubes market is divided into two main types: single-bed and multiple-bed thermal desorption tubes. Single-bed tubes utilize a single chamber for the collection and desorption of volatile compounds, making them suitable for simpler applications. In contrast, multiple-bed tubes feature several chambers, allowing for more complex analyses and the capability to separate different compounds simultaneously. This distinction caters to varying analytical needs in environmental monitoring, industrial processes, and research applications, enhancing operational efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
