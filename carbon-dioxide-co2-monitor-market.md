<p><strong>The Carbon Dioxide (CO2) Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>End-Tidal Carbon Dioxide (EtCO2) Monitors</li><li>Transcutaneous Carbon Dioxide (tcpCO2) Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Carbon Dioxide (CO2) monitor market includes End-Tidal Carbon Dioxide (EtCO2) monitors and Transcutaneous Carbon Dioxide (tcpCO2) monitors. EtCO2 monitors measure the concentration of carbon dioxide at the end of an exhaled breath, crucial for assessing ventilation effectiveness during anesthesia and critical care. In contrast, tcpCO2 monitors assess CO2 levels through the skin, offering continuous, non-invasive monitoring, often used in neonatal and critical care settings. Together, they enhance patient safety and respiratory management in various medical environments.</p></p>
