<p><p>The industrial mining explosives market comprises several key types, including ammonium nitrate explosives (powder), ANFO (ammonium nitrate fuel oil), and emulsion explosives. Ammonium nitrate explosives are used for their high energy and stability, suitable for precise blasting. ANFO, a combination of ammonium nitrate and fuel oil, is cost-effective and widely used due to its simplicity and efficiency. Emulsion explosives, consisting of water-resistant mixtures, offer high performance in wet conditions, making them ideal for diverse mining applications.</p></p>
