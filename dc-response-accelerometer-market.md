<p><p>The DC response accelerometer market can be categorized into three types based on their measurement capabilities: 1-Axis, 2-Axis, and 3-Axis accelerometers. A 1-Axis accelerometer measures acceleration along a single direction, making it suitable for applications focused on specific vertical or horizontal movements. A 2-Axis accelerometer captures acceleration across two perpendicular axes, enhancing motion detection for applications requiring more complexity. Meanwhile, a 3-Axis accelerometer measures acceleration along all three spatial dimensions, providing comprehensive data for advanced motion analysis in various industries.</p></p>
