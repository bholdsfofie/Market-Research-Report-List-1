<p><p>The **Composite Tube Market** includes various types tailored for specific applications. **Round Tubes** are cylindrical, offering uniform strength and used in aerospace, automotive, and sports equipment. **Profile Tubes** have non-circular cross-sections (square, rectangular) for structural applications requiring specific load distribution. **Conical Tubes** taper in diameter, ideal for aerodynamic or weight-saving designs in aerospace and motorsports. **Pre-Preg Tubes** are pre-impregnated with resin, providing high precision and consistency, commonly used in high-performance industries like aerospace and defense. Each type caters to unique structural and functional demands.</p></p>
