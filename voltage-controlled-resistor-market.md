<p><p>The Voltage Controlled Resistor (VCR) market primarily includes two main types: Junction Field Effect Transistors (JFETs) and Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field Effect Transistors (MOSFETs). JFETs operate by varying gate voltage, providing high input impedance and low noise, making them suitable for analog applications. Conversely, MOSFETs offer faster switching speeds and are widely used in digital circuits. Both types are essential for applications in signal processing, analog control systems, and power management, driving their demand across various industries.</p></p>
