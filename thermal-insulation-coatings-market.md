<p><p>Thermal insulation coatings are advanced materials designed to reduce heat transfer, enhancing energy efficiency. The **Isolated Conduction Type** minimizes heat flow through low thermal conductivity, blocking heat transfer via conduction. The **Reflection Type** uses reflective pigments to bounce back radiant heat, reducing heat absorption. The **Radiation Type** emits absorbed heat as infrared radiation, preventing heat buildup. These coatings cater to diverse applications, from buildings to industrial equipment, offering tailored solutions for energy conservation, temperature regulation, and improved thermal performance in various environments.</p></p>
