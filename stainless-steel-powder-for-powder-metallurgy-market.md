<p><p>The stainless steel powder for powder metallurgy market comprises three main types: austenitic, martensitic, and ferritic grades. Austenitic grades offer excellent corrosion resistance and good formability, making them suitable for diverse applications. Martensitic grades are known for their high strength and hardness, commonly used in tools and cutlery. Ferritic grades provide moderate corrosion resistance and good ductility, ideal for automotive and architectural applications. Each grade has unique properties catering to specific industry needs within powder metallurgy.</p></p>
