<p><p>The servo power brakes market comprises three main types: vacuum, pneumatic, and hydraulic servo brakes. Vacuum servo brakes utilize engine-generated vacuum to amplify braking force, enhancing stopping power in conventional vehicles. Pneumatic servo brakes employ compressed air, commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles, providing efficient braking under high load conditions. Hydraulic servo brakes, on the other hand, rely on hydraulic fluid pressure, offering precise control and responsiveness. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to various vehicle requirements and performance standards.</p></p>
