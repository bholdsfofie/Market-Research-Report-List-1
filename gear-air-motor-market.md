<p><ul><li>Leaf Type Air Motor</li><li>Piston Type Air Motor</li><li>Gear Type Pneumatic Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gear Air Motor market includes three main types: Leaf Type Air Motors, Piston Type Air Motors, and Gear Type Pneumatic Motors. Leaf Type Air Motors utilize flexible blades that capture airflow to generate torque, making them efficient for light applications. Piston Type Air Motors employ reciprocating pistons for higher power output, suitable for heavy-duty tasks. Gear Type Pneumatic Motors combine gears with pneumatic power, providing controlled speed and torque, ideal for precision applications in various industries.</p></p>
